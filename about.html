About

Hi, I'm Sharynne. I am a student at the University of Kansas.

I've previously graduated with a bachelor's in Psychology, but I found true passion in programming after taking a class in my last semester before graduation. I fell in love with it so much that I decided to go back for another bachelor's in Computer Science.

There was a sort of satisfaction in seeing things take shape in front of my eyes. With coding, I don't need expensive material or a huge lab. I just need one small machine and I can turn simple lines of code into something amazing for the world.

I find the most interest in web and mobile app development, but in general, I want to create technology that directly helps people. My goal is to not only develop software but to inspire the same curiosity and passion in others. I want to be inspired and to inspire. So, I combined my two passions - helping children and coding - and co-founded DevelopECBC in 2015 to teach kids how to code.

Outside programming, I am a big foodie. I like trying out new restaurants and cooking new foods. Anything from street food to fine dining. You can follow my adventures on Instagram!
