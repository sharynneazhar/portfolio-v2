I am a Computer Science student at the University of Kansas, software engineering intern at Brand New Box, and programming mentor at DevelopECBC. My career goal is to design and develop software that is both innovative and impactful, especially in health and children education.

I found curiosity in programming in my junior year of college in an R-based statistics class. I was so amazed by how a few simple lines of code could turn boring data sets into awesome graphs. Eventually, I wanted to learn more about how programming works fundamentally.

In the next semester, I enrolled in an introductory C++ course. Almost immediately, I knew this is what I wanted to do with my life. I love the combination of creativity and logic involved in writing code. So, long story short, I decided to go back to school for Computer Science. Since then, I have interned with a couple startup companies, founded DevelopECBC, and earned certifications from programming bootcamps and online courses.

Outside programming, I am a big foodie and a gamer. I am also a Youth Leader at Emmanuel Chinese Baptist Church. You can follow my adventures on Instagram!
